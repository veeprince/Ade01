Who is Odedina Adeniyi

UKRN - ICU /Leadership/ Education

Adeniyi Odedina Tumininu is a United Kingdom Registered Nurse (UKRN) and a Pre-assessment Nurse with a passion for educating and helping people achieve their dream life. Words inclusive of, but not limited to, compassionate, kind and gentle may be used to describe him.

He is the founder of Innovateur Global Services. He is currently building an educational team with the aim of helping people obtain life skills and engage them to transform their lives. In the near future, he wishes to practise nursing at a Doctoral level and give back eventually as a professor. He hopes to establish an educational system that will be a reference point to Africa's excellence throughout the world. He also has a passion for studying, meeting people, dancing and travelling. He is a certified result coach.
